AI Applications

How AI is reshaping different industries

Fields of Application

AI is being integrated into almost every sector of the modern economy:

  • Healthcare: AI algorithms can analyze medical images to detect diseases like cancer earlier than human doctors.
  • Finance: Banks use AI to detect fraud in real-time and automate stock trading.
  • Gaming: NPCs (Non-Player Characters) use AI to react intelligently to player actions.
  • Transportation: Self-driving cars (like Tesla) use computer vision to navigate roads safely.
